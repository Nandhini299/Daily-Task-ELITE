PROBABILITY

1. Solution:
   prob = {H,T}
   The prob of getting head = 1/2

2. Solution:
   dice = {1,2,3,4,5,6}, n(p) = 6
   The probability of getting a num greater than 4 = 2/6 => 1/3

3. Solution:
   red = 3 balls, blue = 2 balls, tot = 5 balls
   The prob of getting red balls = num of red balls/ tot balls
                                 = 3/5

4. Solution:
   Two coins = {HH, HT, TH, TT}
   The prob of getting two heads = 1/4 

5. Solution:
   word = ENGINEER , tot = 8 , vowel = 4
   The prob of getting vowel from the word = 4/8 => 1/2
